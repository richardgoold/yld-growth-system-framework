Working Framework

YLD Growth System

Growth is not one activity. It is the system that connects how we understand the market, how we win work, and how we deliver impact for clients. This is a working view of that system - built to start a conversation, not to prescribe an answer.

How to use this
Before the session

Read through each dimension, form a view, and submit your rating and rationale. This is your individual perspective - there are no right answers.

In the session

We will work through each dimension together. Where views differ, that is the most useful part of the conversation.

The rating

Each dimension gets one of three maturity ratings. Be honest - the goal is a shared, accurate picture, not all greens.

The rationale

A sentence or two explaining your rating is enough. What do we have today? What feels inconsistent or unclear?
